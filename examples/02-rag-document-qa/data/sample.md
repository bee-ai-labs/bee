# CloudStore Help Center (Sample Document)

This is a sample knowledge base used by the RAG example. Replace it with your own document to
ask questions about your content.

## Orders and Shipping

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping takes 1–2 business days and costs an
extra $12. You can track any order from the "My Orders" page once it has shipped.

## Refunds

Our refund window is 30 days from the date of purchase. Items must be returned unused and in
their original packaging. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7
business days after we receive the returned item.

## Accounts and Login

If you forget your password, use the "Reset password" link on the sign-in page. A reset email is
sent within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds a one-time code to your login. Enable it under Settings →
Security. We support authenticator apps and SMS codes. If you lose access to your 2FA device,
recovery codes generated during setup can restore access.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions renew automatically each month. You can cancel anytime from Settings → Billing;
cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. There are no cancellation
fees.

## Contact

Support is available Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm. Email support@example.com or use the in-app chat.
